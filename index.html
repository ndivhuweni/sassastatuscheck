R350/370 STATUS CHECK

Here’s how to use this SASSA R350/370 status check tool

1.Enter the ID number you used when applying for the R370 Grant.

2.Enter the phone number you provided during your SASSA R370 registration.

3.click submit button.

After entering the required information, our tool will provide you with your SASSA status, enabling you to view your R350 payment dates.

Here’s how to appeal your SASSA SRD R370 grant if your application has been declined

1.First visit srd.sassa.gov.za/appeals/ when you reach the page, click on the green button that will take you to where you can enter your South African ID number and phone number.

2.After entering the required information, click on the ‘Send Pin’ button. The SASSA organization team will then send a pin. Enter the pin you received on the page and click the submit button.

3.Now you will be able to provide the reason why your R370 SASSA grant was declined.

Then, the SASSA organization team will check if you qualify for the SASSA R370 grant. If you qualify, you will receive it the following month and ensure your bank account has less than R600, or else you won’t receive the payment for that month.

How to change your phone number for SASSA?

  1. First visit the SASSA website srd.sassa.gov.za/sc19/contact
  2. Log in using your ID and application ID.
  3. Look for the option to update your phone details.
  4. Follow the instructions to enter your new phone number.
  5. You might receive requests for additional information or proofs via SMS or email.

If the SASSA official website gives you errors, call the SASSA call center at 0800 60 10 11 and request an agent to update your contact details.

When you check your SASSA status, you’ll see one of these results, each explained below:

  1. Completed Application: You’ve finished applying or reapplying for the SRD R350 grant. Your application will be reviewed each month until it’s approved. This doesn’t mean you’ll get paid yet, but it’s progress.
  2. SASSA Status Declined: Your application didn’t meet the requirements, so SASSA rejected it. They’ll tell you why.
  3. SASSA Status Pending: They got your application but need more time to check it. They’ll update you when it’s done.
  4. Verification of Identity Failed: There was a problem confirming your ID. SASSA needs to match your info with the Department of Home Affairs.
  5. Bank Details Pending: They got your application, but you didn’t give banking info or choose how to get paid. Go to the SASSA website to fix it.
  6. Reapplication In Progress: You haven’t submitted a new application.
  7. Approved Period: This happens for specific months. For example, if it says April is pending, they’re still checking your application for April.
  8. Approved: Your application got approved for a certain month. Look for your payment date in the “payday” field.
  9. Declined: SASSA did not approve your application for a certain month. If you disagree, you can appeal
  10. Canceled: They canceled your R370 grant. You can reinstate your grant by visiting the SASSA OFFICIAL website.

This How you can Cancel Your SASSA Grant:

  1. Go to your nearest SASSA branch office and get a cancellation form.
  2. Fill out the cancellation form.
  3. Attach copies of your ID document and recent bank statements.
  4. Submit the completed cancellation form and documents to SASSA to cancel your grant.

How To Reinstate a Cancelled SASSA Grant:

  1. Everyone goes through ups and downs in life. If your situation has changed and you need assistance again after previously cancelling your SRD grant, you can reinstate it.
  2. Go to the SASSA website and find the “reinstate grant” portal.
  3. Select the reason why you need to reinstate your grant.
  4. Enter the application id you had before.
  5. Submit the reinstatement application form online

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I update my SASSA details?

You can update your Sassa details by requesting a names and surname update on their official website, Sassa: https://srd.sassa.gov.za/said.

How do I appeal a declined SASSA?

You can appeal a declined SASSA grant using the following steps:
1.Visit srd.sassa.gov.za/appeals/ and enter your South African ID and phone number.
2.Click “Send Pin” after entering your information to receive a pin.
3.Enter the received pin and submit.
4.Provide the reason for your declined R370 SASSA grant.
5.SASSA will assess your eligibility for the grant.
6.If eligible, receive the grant the following month; ensure your bank account has less than R600.

Why my R370 SASSA grant declined?

Your SASSA grant was declined because your bank account may have exceeded R600.

How long does R370 appeal take?

An R370 appeal can take 30 to 60 days, depending on when your SASSA team reviews your appeal.
