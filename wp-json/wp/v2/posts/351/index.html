Disability Grant Payment Date for May 2024<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

People who receive the Disability Grant will get their money on Monday, May 6th, 2024. If you get any other grants along with your disability grant, you’ll receive them at the same time.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Children Grant Payment Date for May 2024<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Parents who receive money for their Child Grant will receive their Child Grant payment on Tuesday, May 7th, 2024. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

You can receive your money from this pay points or stores partnered with SASSA, such as Shoprite, Boxer, or Pick n Pay. Just make sure to visit the pay point on your payment date.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
