Before I explain how to apply for the SASSA R370 grant in this article, you must meet certain requirements. You have to be between 18 and 59 years old. You cannot have any other source of income. And you cannot have received any other social grants from SASSA before.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Who Can Apply For The SASSA SRD R370 Grant?<\/strong><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

1) You must be 18 to 59 years old
2) You cannot have any income from a job or other sources
3) You have not gotten other SASSA grants in the past)
4)You shouldn’t receive any other financial aid, like UIF, NSFAS, or bursaries.
5) Your bank account must not contain an amount exceeding R650, as your further payment may be declined<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n

        \n

        • Enter the one-time code: \n
            \n

          • Agree to the terms: \n
              \n

            • Provide your personal details: \n
                \n

              • Share employment status: \n
                  \n
                • You will need to explain your current work and income situation honestly. This determines if you qualify for the grant.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                • Enter banking details: \n
                    \n
