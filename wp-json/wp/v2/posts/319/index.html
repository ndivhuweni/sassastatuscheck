How much is SASSA child grant 2024?<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Sassa has announced that the child grant has increased to R530 for children under the age of 18.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Who qualifies for SASSA child grant?<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Those who qualify for the SASSA child grant are children under the age of 18, and their parents must not be working for the government. To qualify, you need to be a South African citizen. Single parent’ yearly income can’t exceed R52,800. If you’re married, the total income for both of you shouldn’t go over R105,600 per year. Both you and your child must live in South Africa.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
