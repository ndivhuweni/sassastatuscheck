The Simple Way On How to Apply for R370 Grant

  • Post author:ndivhu
  • Post published:May 2, 2024
  • Post category:Sassa R350 Grant

Before I explain how to apply for the SASSA R370 grant in this article, you must meet certain requirements. You have to be between 18 and 59 years old. You cannot have any other source of income. And you cannot have received any other social grants from SASSA before.

Who Can Apply For The SASSA SRD R370 Grant?

1) You must be 18 to 59 years old
2) You cannot have any income from a job or other sources
3) You have not gotten other SASSA grants in the past)
4)You shouldn’t receive any other financial aid, like UIF, NSFAS, or bursaries.
5) Your bank account must not contain an amount exceeding R650, as your further payment may be declined

How to Apply for R370 Grant

  1. First Visit the SASSA official website: srd.sassa.gov.za.
    • Click on the first step to apply online.
  2. Start your application:
    • Look for a button that says “click here to apply online” and click it. This will take you to the application page.
  3. Enter your cell phone number:
    • You will need to type in your mobile number. After entering it, click “Send SMS”. This verifies it is your real number.
  4. Enter the one-time code:
    • You will get a special code (called an OTP) sent to your phone via text message. Enter this code on the website to continue. This code helps keep your application secure.
  5. Agree to the terms:
    • You must read and agree to the terms and conditions. Confirm that you accept them to move forward.
  6. Provide your personal details:
    • The form will ask for your name, ID number, and other personal information. Double-check that everything is correct.
  7. Share employment status:
    • You will need to explain your current work and income situation honestly. This determines if you qualify for the grant.
  8. Enter banking details:
    • Provide your bank account information correctly. This is where the grant money would be deposited if approved.
  9. Complete the full application:
    • Follow all the remaining instructions carefully. Review everything before submitting.
  10. Wait for the outcome:
    • Once submitted, you will get a confirmation message. SASSA will then review your application, which may take some time. They will contact you with the final decision.

How to Apply for R370 Grant via WhatsApp

To apply via WhatsApp:

  1. Message “Hi” to SASSA’s WhatsApp number: 082 046 8553.
  2. Follow the instructions provided.
  3. Submit your application directly through WhatsApp.
  4. Receive confirmation of submission.

Who is eligible for the SASSA SRD R370 Grant?

1) You must be 18 to 59 years old
2) You cannot have any income from a job or other sources
3) You have not gotten other SASSA grants in the past
4)You shouldn’t receive any other financial aid, like UIF, NSFAS, or bursaries.
5) Your bank account must not contain an amount exceeding R650, as your further payment may be declined.

How do I check the R370 status?

To check your SASSA R370 payment date for 2024, visit https://350statuscheck.org.za/.

Can I apply R370 Grant for someone else?

Yes, you can apply for the R370 Grant on behalf of someone else, but make sure you gather all the required information, such as their ID number and other personal details. Also, ensure you have permission to apply for them. Accuracy is crucial to avoid any issues with the application.
